Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

PetScore is an AI-powered pet product compatibility tool operated as an independent UK digital product. By using PetScore, you agree to these terms.

What data we collect

PetScore stores the following data locally in your browser only:

  • Your pet's profile (type, breed, age, size, health conditions)
  • Your report history and journal entries
  • Your API key (stored only in your browser, never sent to our servers)

If you subscribe to our email newsletter, we collect your email address for the sole purpose of sending pet product tips. We do not share your email with third parties.

How we use your data

Pet profile data is sent to Google's Gemini AI API to generate compatibility scores. This data is processed according to Google's privacy policy. We do not store your pet data on our servers — it exists only in your browser's local storage.

AI-generated content disclaimer

PetScore uses artificial intelligence to generate product compatibility scores and recommendations. These are provided for general guidance only and do not constitute veterinary advice. Always consult a qualified veterinarian before making health-related decisions for your pet.

Scores and recommendations are based on general breed knowledge and may not account for your pet's individual circumstances. PetScore accepts no liability for decisions made based on AI-generated content.

Subscriptions & payments

PetScore subscriptions are processed securely via Gumroad. We do not store payment details. Subscriptions renew monthly at the stated price. You may cancel at any time via your Gumroad account. Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase in accordance with UK consumer law.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. As most data is stored locally in your browser, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage. For email data, contact us to request deletion.

Cookies

PetScore uses browser local storage (not cookies) to save your preferences and data locally on your device. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of PetScore after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related queries, please contact us via our website.
